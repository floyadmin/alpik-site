About alpiK

alpiK is a team of professional rope access specialists focused on high‑level works of any complexity.

We work where standard methods are inaccessible or inefficient, ensuring high quality, safety and clear deadlines.

Our company combines experience, modern technology and a responsible approach to every project.

We don’t work by templates — we tailor each solution to the client’s needs and support the project from idea to final result.

What do we do?

We provide a full range of rope access and industrial works, including:

Installation of gutter systems and flashings

Chimney stack repair and modernization

Installation of chimneys and air ducts

Demolition of chimneys and steel structures

Grain elevator overhaul, silo cleaning and repair

Anti‑corrosion treatment and painting of steel structures

Sandblasting and high‑pressure surface cleaning

Installation of translucent structures

Installation of advertising structures, banner hanging

Roof cleaning from snow, icicles and ice

Washing facades, windows and industrial premises

Decorations and lighting installation

Solar panel installation

Routing and installation of power cables at height
